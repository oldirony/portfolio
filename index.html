Hi! I am Raffaele

I’m a young Italian web developer and I love coding and building websites. I earned a Bachelor’s degree in Communication Design at Polytechnic University of Milan in 2012. For two years I worked in a communication agency. I have knowledge of web languages such as html, css, javascript, PHP, SQL and techniques such as SEO, mobile optimization, UI & UX improvement.

Bored by the static Italian enviroment, I decided to move to London in September 2014 where I have been hired as a Front-End Developer by a prestigious School of English. I coded the website for this school, side by side with a great developer and a famous designer.

After the publishing of the website (and some good, old, live corrections), I projected and developed a webapp for doing and marking the online student initial test.
