Hi! I am Raffaele

I'm a 25 years old Italian web developer and I love coding and building websites adn webapp. I earned a Bachelor’s degree in Communication Design at Polytechnic University of Milan in 2012. For two years I worked in a communication agency. I have knowledge of web languages such as html, css, javascript, PHP, SQL and techniques such as SEO, mobile optimization, UI & UX improvement.

Bored by the static Italian environment, I decided to move to London in September 2014 where I have been hired as a Front-End Developer by a prestigious School of English. I coded the website for this school and a webapp for the online tests.

Since May 2015 I've been working as frontend and backend developer for STEEL London, growing my knowledge of new technologies and techniques as well as team working and AGILE workflow. I created websites based on the Silverstripe Framework, realised several Angular applications and managed remote servers.
